Harry and Harriet’s Hang-gliding Haven

Company Story and History

Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven is a renowned company specializing in providing premier products and services catering to the hang-gliding and skydiving community. Founded with a passion for adventure sports, the company has established itself as a leading authority in the industry. Originated in 2000, it has already served over 10,000 customers and has even been awarded "Best Adventure Sports Company". With a rich history built on a foundation of passion, dedication, and excellence, Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven continues to inspire thrill-seekers and adventurers to push their limits and experience the exhilaration of flight.

Company Mission: To provide high-quality products and services for hang-gliding and skydiving enthusiasts.

Company History: Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven traces its roots back to the year 2000 when Harry Smith and Harriet Jones, both ardent hang-gliders, joined forces to establish their dream venture. Fuelled by their shared passion for adventure sports, they envisioned a company that would not only provide top-notch hang-gliding equipment but also offer comprehensive training and unforgettable experiences for enthusiasts. In its early stages, the company operated from a small storefront, serving a local clientele with personalized attention and care. As the years progressed, Harry and Harriet's dedication and commitment to excellence propelled the company forward. In 2005, recognizing the growing demand for adventure sports, they expanded their services to include skydiving equipment and training, further solidifying their position in the industry. Today, with a rich history spanning over two decades, Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven stands as a beacon of innovation and adventure in the world of hang-gliding and skydiving. From its humble beginnings to its current status as a leading authority in the industry, the company continues to inspire thrill-seekers worldwide to embark on exhilarating adventures and experience the freedom of flight.
